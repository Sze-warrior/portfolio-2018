Zhuo Photography App:

In April 2014, Apptech Solutions approached me to do user-testing and mobile interface design work using their custom software. I was asked to identify a problem in the market that could use a new application and build a functioning prototype with Apptech Solutions software.

A problem I identified in the market is the lack of communication for clients to see when their favourite Photography company is booked out. I designed an application for a fictitious company called Zhuo Photography - a photography company specializing in various photography services. The application would provide users an option to book the companies services on a date that is free, a custom map of their location, a list of their events and a vouchers page.

The design work I completed included the User-Interface Design, User-Experience Design, Wireframing, Graphics/Branding and Photo-Editing for this design project while the development was done in Apptechs software.
