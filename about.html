About me:

I am an Digital Designer who primarily specialises in Interaction Design and Front End Development.

I have been creating user-flow digrams, looking up web components on online design systems like Google Material Design, designing layouts inspired from Dribbble and coding up UI animations and interactions with help from sites like CSS Tricks, Treehouse and Stackoverflow for the majority of my career and have enjoyed all the milestones along the way.

Current skillset:

Through work experience, colleauges and communities I have been a part of in my career, I have been fortunate enough to gain insights and learnings from different areas to apply to all my projects. Outside of work I regularly experiment to expand my skillset and opening up my mind to new technologies and processes as well.

Some of the most prominant skills I have displayed in my work include:

  • Interaction Design
  • Web Design
  • UI Design and UI animation
  • User Experience Design
  • Front End Web Development languages (HTML, CSS and JavaScript)
  • SVGs/XML and SVG animations
  • Git and Github
  • Design Systems and Styleguides
  • Icon Design
  • Rapid prototyping
  • Sublime and Sourcetree
