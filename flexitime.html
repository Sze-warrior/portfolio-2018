Flexitime website design:

FlexiTime is a software as a service start-up based in Wellington. FlexiTime builds software to help businesses better manage staff, payroll and rosters. During my time here, I worked on the launch and redesign of their new website from October 2014 to January 2015.

My roles with FlexiTime included Web Design, Graphic Design, Front End HTML and CSS development in Wordpress and collaborating with FlexiTime's Chief Marketing Officer on how to improve the online User-Experience.
