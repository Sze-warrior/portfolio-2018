Second Year homepage:

Second Year was a job listing website concept aimed primarily at travellers looking for one off or temp. employment opportunities.

I was approached to design a responsive home page for this concept for development to begin in the near future to help improve the lives of travellers looking for employment opportunities.

In this role, I applied my skills primarily in Responsive Web Design. Unfortunatly due to budgeting constraints and limited resources, the project was put on hold until a future date.

Styleguide:

Although the project was cancelled, I produced a starting set of styles to help whoever carries on with the project again to maintain consistency with the websites colors and typography styling.
